Convert seconds to years.

1 Earth Year = 31557600 seconds in a year

1/31557600 Earth Year = 1 second

Mercury
-------
1 Earth Year = 31557600 seconds in a year

1 Mercury Year = 0.2408467 Earth Year

1 Mercury Year = 31557600 * 0.2408467 seconds in a Mercury year

1 Mercury Year = 7,600,543.81992 seconds in a year

1/(31557600 * 0.2408467) Mercury Year = 1 second in a Mercury year
